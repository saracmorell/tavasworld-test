Writing

Dive into the enchanting world of The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas — a heartwarming tale that teaches young readers the power of life lessons, kindness, and friendship. Join Raspen, Sylas, and their unexpected new friendships on a journey through a serene forest filled with redwoods, bubbling rivers, and valuable life lessons. Through vibrant storytelling and charming illustrations, Sara inspires emotional growth and resilience in children ages 1–6. With each adventure, a lesson they find, Raspen and Sylas grow wise and kind. They show young hearts it’s okay to feel, and that sharing emotions helps us heal.
